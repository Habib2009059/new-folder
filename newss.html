Sri Lanka make 381 after Nissanka double ton against Afghanistan

1st ODI

Pathum Nissanka became the first Sri Lankan to score an ODI double century, powering the hosts to an immense 381 against Afghanistan at Pallekele in their series opener on Friday. The opener finished unbeaten on 210 to break the island nation's longstanding record of 189 by Sanath Jayasuriya against India in 2000. It was also the joint fifth-highest individual score in ODIs.

No allocation for research in 15pc private varsities

Updated: 09 Feb 2024, 19: 33

UGC Annual Report-2022

Although research is one of the major responsibilities of a university, a concerning trend of ignoring this responsibility persists in some universities across the country. Some 15 per cent of private universities, including some old ones, did not have allocations of a single penny for research in 2022. A similar situation was found in some newly established public universities. Conversely, the prominent universities kept comparatively higher allocations for research, and it is reflected in their overall academic performance, according to the University Grants Commission’s (UGC) annual report for 2022.
